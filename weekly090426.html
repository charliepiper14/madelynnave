Above Target: Account is Working

The account delivered 3.76x blended ROAS this week, above the 3.5x target, with purchases nearly doubling from 16 to 31. Spend was up 14.1% week on week but revenue grew 53% and purchases grew 94%, so efficiency is broadly holding. The prior week ROAS of 4.16x was on a much smaller $5,564 spend base and was influenced by high-value individual orders. At $9,427 spend this week, the 3.76x represents a more meaningful read of account health. Three of four active ad sets are at or above target ROAS.

The One Problem: TLIMS CBO Dragged by Eden Learning Phase

TLIMS CBO is running at 1.71x blended ROAS this week. The drag is entirely the Eden Collection ad set at 0.33x on $1,676 spend. This ad set is in learning phase and its video creatives are absorbing most of the budget with zero conversions. The TLIMS CBO Value Optimisation ad set underneath it is performing at 3.43x with 7 purchases and $217 CPA. The structural issue is ad-level creative mix inside Eden, not the campaign or ad set architecture. This is fixable with ad-level pauses today.
