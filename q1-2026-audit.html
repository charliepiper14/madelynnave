What the Data Shows

  • However CPA is $473 vs $266, nearly double
  • High ATC and checkout volume suggests purchase intent exists
  • TLIMS Brand Logo ad within Combined is the account's best performing single ad at 17.5x ROAS

Key Problem Identified

The Combined ad set has 28 purchases from $13.2K spend. The TLIMS Brand Logo alone is delivering exceptional efficiency (17.5x ROAS, $178 spend for 1 purchase). The issue is the volume of zero-return ads running alongside it in the same combined ad set are dragging down blended CPA. The hypothesis is sound. The execution needs tightening.

Recommended Action

  • Continue test but pause zero-purchase ads within Combined
  • Consolidate budget onto top 3 to 4 performing creatives in the combined ad set
  • Target: bring Combined CPA below $300 before scaling past $500/day
  • Revisit test verdict at end of March with clean data

Oscar's Cross-Category Hypothesis: Still Valid

Oscar's original insight that customers enter through Christian art but purchase across both categories remains strategically correct. The Combined test has 237 adds-to-cart and 28 purchases, showing genuine buying behaviour. The TLIMS Brand Logo delivering 17.5x ROAS in the combined structure validates the concept. The path forward is creative consolidation, not campaign shutdown.
